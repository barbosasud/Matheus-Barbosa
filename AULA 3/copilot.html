Ayrton Senna

Tragically, Ayrton Senna's life was cut short when he died in an accident while leading the 1994 San Marino Grand Prix. His death had a profound impact on the sport, leading to increased safety measures in Formula One. Senna's legacy lives on, and he remains a beloved figure in Brazil and among motorsport fans worldwide.
